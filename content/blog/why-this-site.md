---
title: "Why this site exists"
date: "2026-05-16"
summary: "A small, fast portfolio for architecture notes, employment context, and GitHub-backed work."
---

I wanted one place that loads quickly, reads cleanly on mobile, and separates **public narrative** from **private drafts** (those stay in my career workspace).

This stack is intentionally boring on purpose: **Next.js**, **Markdown posts**, typed **data files** for roles and projects. That keeps shipping friction low while I iterate on inbound copy.

If you landed here from Upwork or LinkedIn: the fastest signal is usually **Experience** plus links out to **GitHub** once repos are public.
